The Segmenting Principle 📁

Chunk the material into small “bites."

Think about binders with tabbed dividers. What content could go in one tab? Think of headings as these binder tabs.

Application ⚙️

Adding section headers is a simple way to break up text and chunk sections. The image below does not include headers. Hover over or tap it to see the difference when section headers are added.
