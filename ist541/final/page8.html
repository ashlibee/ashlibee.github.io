The Personalization Principle 😎

Use a human voice. Include a human/human-like coach. Be kind with your words.

"The feeling of social presence . . . causes the learner to engage in deeper cognitive processing during learning . . . , which results in a better learning outcome."

(Clark & Mayer, p. 201, 2016).

Application ⚙️

💡 Bitmojis are a fun, easy way to create humanized personalization in Canvas courses. Use the Google Chrome extension to quickly copy and paste Bitmojis into your courses.
